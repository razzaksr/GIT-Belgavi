Functional Requirements Specification (FRS)

Project: Insurance Policy Tracker

1. Purpose

The Insurance Policy Tracker manages policyholder records, calculates premiums, and tracks claim eligibility.

2. Scope

Maintain insurance policy records.

Perform CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete).

Calculate premium schedules and claim eligibility.

Provide filtering by premium, sum assured, and term.

3. Functional Requirements

Create Policy: Add policyholder details (ID, name, premium, sum assured, term, type).

Read Policy: Retrieve policy details by ID.

Update Policy: Modify policy details and recalculate premium.

Delete Policy: Remove policy record.

Calculate Premium: Compute premium due dates and amounts.

Filter Policies: Query policies by premium, sum assured, or term.

4. Non-Functional Requirements

Data persistence using JSON or Pickle.

Secure handling of policyholder data.

Extensible design for future modules (e.g., claims management).

5. Users

Insurance agents

Policyholders (view-only access)

6. Constraints

Must adhere to insurance regulatory standards.

Should support multiple policy types.
